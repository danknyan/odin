I'm a data scientist and applied AI/NLP engineer currently based in Jakarta, Indonesia, with a Master of Engineering in Data Science from the University of Sydney.

My background has always centered on language and its impact. I spent years in project management for education and corporate training in Japan and Canada before moving into data science and NLP. Experience in PM and data shapes how I work now: whether an analysis is *usable* by the people who need it and presented in a context relevant to them is as important as the modeling itself.

Recently, that's meant building an end-to-end NLP pipeline for global news topic modeling (hierarchical topic modeling + LLM-based labeling, deployed as an interactive dashboard), and applying Vision Transformers to plant disease diagnosis on real-world field imagery.
