I'm a data scientist and applied AI/NLP engineer based in Jakarta, Indonesia, nearly completed my Master of Engineering in Data Science at the University of Sydney.

My background has always centered on language and communication. I spent years in education and corporate training consulting in Japan and Canada before moving into data science, and that experience shapes how I work now: I care as much about whether an analysis is *usable* by the people who need it as I do about the modeling itself.

Recently, that's meant building an end-to-end NLP pipeline for global news topic modeling (hierarchical topic modeling + LLM-based labeling, deployed as an interactive dashboard), and applying Vision Transformers to plant disease diagnosis on real-world field imagery.

I hold certifications in IBM Data Science, Natural Language Processing, Google Advanced Data Analytics, and IBM AI Engineering, and have professional working proficiency in Japanese and French.
